About Spotify Analytics

Our Mission

Spotify Analytics is dedicated to providing music enthusiasts with deep insights into their listening habits. We transform raw data from Spotify into meaningful visualizations and analytics that help users understand their musical tastes and discover new music.

Our platform combines modern data analytics with a user-friendly interface to make music analytics accessible and enjoyable for everyone, from casual listeners to music professionals.

Why We Built This

We believe music preferences reveal fascinating insights about ourselves and the world around us. By analyzing listening patterns, we can discover trends, understand emotion connections, and find hidden gems in the vast ocean of music available today.

Features

Genre Analysis

Discover how your music taste breaks down across different genres, with beautiful pie charts and distribution graphs.

Audio Features

Analyze the technical aspects of your music like danceability, energy, valence, and tempo with detailed radar charts.

Time Comparison

Track how your music preferences have evolved over time, from recent favorites to all-time classics.

Privacy & Security

Your Data is Safe

We take your privacy seriously. We only request read-only access to your Spotify data and never store your personal information on our servers. All analysis is performed in real-time, and your data is never shared with third parties.

Secure Authentication

We use Spotify's official OAuth 2.0 authentication process, which means we never see or store your Spotify password. You can revoke access at any time through your Spotify account settings.
